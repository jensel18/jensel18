## 🚀 About Me 
I am an Operations Leader and data analyst with a strong passion for helping organizations with people development, derive meaningful value from their data. I specialize in upskilling team members, transforming complex and unstructured datasets into clear, insightful narratives that support informed decision-making.

My expertise includes developing user-focused dashboards, automating repetitive processes to enhance team efficiency, and building analytical models that enable organizations to plan strategically for the future.

When I'm not working with data, I enjoy playing video games, going out with my family and enjoying nature. I love the "aha!" moment when data reveals something new and useful.
